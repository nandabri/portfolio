Highlighted Projects

  • Network Infrastructure – Network Infrastructure Management System: A project focused on managing a company's network infrastructure, including mapping departments, connected devices, and controlling IT resources across headquarters and branch offices.
  • Bibliotech – Library Book Rental Management: A system designed for managing the rental and return of books in libraries. It enables efficient control of borrowings, reservations, and user history.
  • Neurolink – Mental Health Clinic Appointment App: An application developed to schedule appointments at a mental health clinic. It allows patients to choose professionals, book time slots, and track their consultation history.
  • Business Intelligence – Humanitarian NGO – A data analysis (BI) system for a non-governmental organization, focused on identifying poverty areas, tracking donations, and managing items needed for humanitarian campaigns.
  • Cresça – Child Development Monitoring Platform: A platform for tracking child development up to 6 years old. It collects and analyzes cognitive, motor, and social data, supporting educators and guardians in monitoring children's progress.
  • PetShop Management System – This project is a distributed system designed for managing clients, services, and grooming appointments (bathing and trimming) in pet shops. The system is intended exclusively for internal use by the staff, providing a practical and accessible solution directly via smartphone or tablet.
